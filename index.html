About

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. Known as the Missile Man of India, he was instrumental in India’s space and defense programs, and a beloved figure for his dedication to education and youth empowerment.

Legacy

  • Developed India’s missile program (AGNI, PRITHVI).
  • Key figure in the 1998 Pokhran nuclear tests.
  • Served as India's 11th President (2002–2007).
  • Wrote inspirational books like Wings of Fire & Ignited Minds.
